FAQ
Questions? Answered.
Do I need technical skills to use Wavo?
Not at all. Wavo is built for non-technical solo entrepreneurs. If you can use Gmail, you can use Wavo. Setup takes under 5 minutes.
How are "agent tasks" counted?
Each meaningful action an agent takes — sending an email, writing a report, analyzing data — counts as one task. Browsing and reading doesn't count.
Can I customize what each agent does?
Yes. On Pro and Scale plans you can give each agent custom instructions, set their tone, restrict or expand their scope, and define how they prioritize tasks.
Is my data safe?
Absolutely. Wavo uses end-to-end encryption, never trains on your data, and is fully SOC 2 compliant. Your business data stays yours.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. No lock-ins, no cancellation fees. Downgrade to free or cancel with one click from your settings.
